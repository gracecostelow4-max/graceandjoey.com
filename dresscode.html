Semi-Formal Garden

Although this is not a black-tie event, we'd love to see everyone dressed up and looking their best! Since our wedding is in June, we reccomend breathable fabrics like silk, linen, and cotton to help you stay cool and comfortable.

Ladies

Ladies are welcome to wear maxi or midi-length dresses. Block heels or wedges are encouraged for the grass.

Gentlemen

Gentlemen are welcome to wear button up shirts with slacks or a full suit.

Colors

Dusty Rose | Blush Pink | Mauve | Sage Green | Olive Green | Browns

To help keep a cohesive and beautiful look for our celebration we kindly ask that guests try to stay within our color scheme of varying shades of dusty rose, blush pink, sage green, olive green and other shades of earthy browns. Floral designs are also encouraged. Think of colors that would be in a garden! Thank you!
